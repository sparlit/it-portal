# Homepage Structure & Copy Strategy

## 1. Homepage Structure (The Conversion Path)

### I. Hero Section (The Hook)
*   **Content**: Bold headline, sub-headline, primary CTA, and a high-quality visual of pristine garments.
*   **Why**: You have 8 seconds to capture attention. This section defines who we are and why the visitor should care immediately.

### II. Trust Section (The Authority)
*   **Content**: Logos of corporate clients, certifications (ISO), and "As Seen In" Qatar media.
*   **Why**: Social proof reduces the "stranger danger" and establishes professional credibility, especially for corporate contracts.

### III. Services Section (The Solution)
*   **Content**: 4 distinct blocks for Washing & Pressing, Dry Cleaning, Pressing Only, and Tailoring.
*   **Why**: Visitors need to quickly confirm we offer exactly what they need. Clear categorization reduces cognitive load.

### IV. Social Proof Section (The Validation)
*   **Content**: Real customer testimonials and a 5-star rating summary.
*   **Why**: People trust other people more than brands. Seeing success stories from public and corporate clients builds confidence.

### V. FAQ Section (The Objection Crusher)
*   **Content**: 5-6 common questions about pricing, turnaround time, and Qatar-wide delivery.
*   **Why**: Proactively answering questions removes friction in the buying process and shows transparency.

### VI. Final Call to Action (The Closer)
*   **Content**: A final, high-energy prompt to book or contact for corporate rates.
*   **Why**: Never leave a visitor wondering "what's next?" This section captures anyone who scrolled to the bottom.

"Get your laundry back perfectly cleaned and pressed within 24 hours—without the premium price tag or the hassle of leaving your home."
