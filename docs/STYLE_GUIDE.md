# Al Rayes Laundry - Visual Style Guide

## 1. Brand Essence
**Modern | Luxury | Trustworthy**
The brand should feel as clean as the clothes we deliver. We use high contrast, generous spacing, and a palette that evokes water, cleanliness, and precision.

## 2. Color Palette (Public Portal)
*   **Primary Blue**: `#0F172A` (Deep Navy - Authority & Luxury)
*   **Accent Blue**: `#3B82F6` (Electric Blue - Modernity & Water)
*   **Success Green**: `#10B981` (Freshness & Cleanliness)
*   **Background**: `#F8FAFC` (Cool White - Airy & Crisp)
*   **Text (Main)**: `#1E293B` (Soft Charcoal - Readability)

## 3. Typography (Free Google Fonts)
*   **Heading Font**: `Inter` (Bold, 700-900 weight) - Modern, clean, and highly legible.
*   **Body Font**: `Plus Jakarta Sans` (Regular, 400 weight) - Elegant, professional, and slightly unique.
*   **Pairing Strategy**: Use Inter for all H1-H4 tags for a "heavy" industrial feel. Use Plus Jakarta Sans for paragraphs to provide a "soft" luxury touch.

## 4. Button Styles
*   **Primary Button**:
    - Background: `#3B82F6` (Electric Blue)
    - Text: `#FFFFFF` (White)
    - Border Radius: `8px` (Slightly rounded, modern)
    - Hover Effect: Darken by 10% and lift slightly (-2px translateY).
*   **Secondary Button**:
    - Background: Transparent
    - Border: `2px solid #0F172A`
    - Text: `#0F172A`
    - Hover Effect: Fill background with `#0F172A` and change text to white.

## 5. Spacing & Layout
*   **Section Gaps**: `96px` to `128px` (Large gaps create a "Luxury" feel).
*   **Container**: Max-width `1280px` with `24px` side padding for mobile.
*   **Grid**: 12-column system for desktop, 1-column for mobile.

## 6. Image Direction
*   **Subject**: Close-ups of fabric textures (silk, linen), professional staff in crisp uniforms, and modern laundry machinery.
*   **Style**: Bright, high-key lighting, minimal background clutter, slight blue/cool tint.
*   **FOSS Resource**: Use Unsplash or Pexels for high-quality, free imagery.

## 7. Adaptation for Portals
Each of the 15 portals follows the same spacing and typography rules but swaps the **Primary Blue** (`#0F172A`) and **Accent Blue** (`#3B82F6`) for their specific departmental colors (e.g., Emerald for Sales, Amber for Production) as defined in the Architecture Plan.
